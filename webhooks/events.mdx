---

Webhooks provide a powerful tool for keeping track of important events related to your company and its users. By setting up webhooks, you can be instantly notified when a user purchases your product, when they should be granted access to your software, or when their access should be revoked.

Your application's role is simple - listen for incoming webhook requests from Whop and process them according to your needs.

Here's an overview of the different webhook events that Whop sends:

- [membership.went_valid](/webhooks/events#membership-went-valid)
- [membership.went_invalid](/webhooks/events#membership-went-invalid)
- [membership.metadata_updated](/webhooks/events#membership-metadata-updated)
- [payment.succeeded](/webhooks/events#payment-succeeded)
- [payment.failed](/webhooks/events#payment-failed)
- [payment.affiliate_reward_created](/webhooks/events#payment-affiliate-reward-created)
